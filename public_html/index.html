How Did I End Up In Computer Science?

When I started my time at GMU, I had no idea what I wanted to even do after High School (as many other prospective students do). During the summer after High School had ended, I decided to take a free Python course where I had learned some very basic operations. Mind you, majoring in Computer Science did not cross my mind at this moment. Fast foward a year into my Undergraduate: I was still undeclared, and had to choose a major to begin making actual progress. My friend, Nabil Darwich, was majoring in Computer Science at the time, and he would tell me about what he would be doing in his CS classes occasionally. Hearing about what he was learning excited me. Me taking the python course over the summer a year earlier and Nabil's talks with me pushed me to pursue Computer Science as my career choice.
